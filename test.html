UniChat

Our Goal

Our goal is to develop UniChat, an AI-powered chatbot that enhances how students, faculty, and staff at Carleton University access institutional information.
We aim to provide fast, accurate, and trustworthy answers through an intuitive conversational interface that simplifies navigating university resources. By leveraging modern AI and retrieval technologies, UniChat is designed to deliver reliable and explainable responses that users can confidently depend on. Ultimately, our mission is to improve accessibility, save time, and create a more efficient and user-friendly way for the university community to find the information they need.
